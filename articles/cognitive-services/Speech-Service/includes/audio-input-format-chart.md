## Supported audio input format

The Speech SDK uses the following format for audio input.

| Format | Codec | Bitrate | Sample Rate | Channels |
|--------|-------|---------|-------------|----------|
| WAV | PCM | 16-bit | 8 kHz or 16 kHz | 1 (mono) |
