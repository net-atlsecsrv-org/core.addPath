The default audio streaming format is WAV (16 KHz, 16-bit, and mono PCM). Outside of WAV / PCM, the following codec compressed input formats are also supported:

- MP3
- OPUS/OGG
- FLAC
- ALAW in wav container
- MULAW in wav container
